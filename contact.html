Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can you start a project?

We can typically start projects within 1-2 weeks, depending on requirements and team availability. Emergency projects can be expedited with proper arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, wire transfers, and other standard payment methods. Custom payment arrangements can be negotiated for enterprise clients.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We provide comprehensive post-launch support including maintenance, updates, bug fixes, and technical assistance as part of our service commitment.

Can you work with existing codebases?

Absolutely. We frequently work with legacy systems and can integrate with existing infrastructure, refactor code, or migrate applications as needed.

What about data security and compliance?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations. Data encryption and regular security audits are standard.

Do you offer customized solutions?

Yes, all our solutions are customized to meet your specific business needs. We work closely with you to understand requirements and deliver tailored solutions.

What if I need to scale my application?

Our architecture is designed for scalability. We can handle increases in traffic, users, and data volumes without compromising performance or reliability.

Can you provide references from past clients?

Of course. We have many satisfied clients across various industries. We're happy to provide references and case studies upon request.

What are your service areas?

We serve clients worldwide across North America, Europe, Asia, and beyond. Our team is distributed to provide global coverage and local expertise.
